Frequently Asked Questions

1. How far in advance do I need to book to guarantee same-day delivery?

Same-day delivery is available in most service areas when you book before late morning. For the best chance at a specific delivery window, earlier in the day is always better. Next-day delivery is available for bookings made at any point, and we'll confirm your window at the time of booking.

2. How is the container placed on my property and does the driver need access instructions?

Our drivers assess placement on arrival and position the container to maximize loading access while minimizing disruption to your driveway, landscaping, or access points. If you have specific placement requirements — proximity to a garage, clearance from a fence line, or distance from a doorway — let us know when you book and we'll communicate that directly to the driver before delivery.

3. What if my project generates more waste than the container holds?

If the container fills before your project wraps up, contact us and we'll arrange a swap — removing the full container and replacing it with an empty one. Swap pricing is straightforward and discussed upfront so there are no surprises if it becomes necessary. It's always better to call early rather than overfill the container beyond its capacity.

4. Is green dumpster rental available at the same price as standard service?

Green dumpster rental is priced competitively with our standard service, and in many cases the cost difference is minimal. Pricing depends on container size and the composition of the material being collected. When you call to book, let us know you're interested in environmentally responsible disposal and we'll provide a specific quote for that service alongside our standard rate so you can make an informed decision.

5. Do I need a permit to place the dumpster on the street?

Permit requirements depend on your specific location and local municipality rules. Generally, placing a bin on a private driveway does not require a permit, but street placement often does. We can guide you on the necessary steps to obtain one if needed.
